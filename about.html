CASAAID History

CASAAID (China Admission Scholarship Agency Aid) was established in June 2016 with the aim of connecting international students with Chinese tertiary education and cultural exchange. We offer services in university admission, scholarship grants, career guidance as well as working permints.

Our vision is to empower foreign students, African students in particular with the financial aid they might need to further perpetuate their tertiary studies. CASAAID your one stop solution for studying abroad in CHINA.
